What is OSINT?

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) is the practice of collecting and analyzing publicly available information to answer specific questions. From tracking down missing persons to verifying facts, OSINT skills are used by journalists, investigators, cybersecurity professionals, and researchers worldwide. It's detective work for the digital age.

OSINT Skills You'll Master

  • → Social Media Intelligence — Track profiles and map connections across platforms
  • → Geolocation Techniques — Pinpoint locations from photos and videos
  • → Metadata Analysis — Extract hidden information from digital files
  • → Data Correlation — Connect dots across multiple data sources
  • → Digital Forensics — Verify information and detect manipulation
  • → Investigation Methodology — Build systematic approaches to research
